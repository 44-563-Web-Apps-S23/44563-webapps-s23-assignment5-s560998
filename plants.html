ABUTILON

Abutilon is a large genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae. It is distributed throughout the tropics and subtropics of the Americas, Africa, Asia, and Australia

Abutilon Wikipedia

KING PALM

The king palm (Archontophoenix cunninghamiana) is a tropical plant native to Australia. It can grow outdoors in U.S. Department of Agriculture plant hardiness zones 9b through 11, but in cooler areas, it must be planted in containers and moved indoors for the winter and will not tolerate temperatures below 28 degrees Fahrenheit.

King Palm Wikipedia
